Our Story

Sammy’s Carpentry Services began with a deep commitment to strong craftsmanship, reliable service, and high-quality carpentry work. What started as a small local operation has grown into one of Bridgeport, CT’s most trusted names for professional carpentry, custom woodwork, and long lasting home improvements.

From custom built carpentry projects and precision woodworking to repairs, renovations, and structural upgrades, our mission has always been clear deliver honest pricing, dependable service, and craftsmanship that stands the test of time. Our team has earned strong community trust by consistently providing exceptional results for homeowners and local businesses.

Today, we continue to bring experience, skill, and modern carpentry techniques to every job. At Sammy’s Carpentry Services, we’re more than carpenters we’re dedicated partners in building, improving, and maintaining the spaces that matter most.
